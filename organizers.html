Workshop Team

The organizing team brings together expertise in computer vision, data mining, plant phenotyping, remote sensing, and digital agriculture.

Zijian Wang

ARC Early Career Industry Fellow and Lecturer at The University of Queensland, Australia. His research focuses on multimedia analysis and model generalisation in computer vision, with work in NeurIPS, CVPR, ICCV, ACM Multimedia, and IEEE TPAMI.

Shijie Wang

Research Fellow with the School of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science, University of Queensland. His research interests include image processing, computer vision, and deep learning.

Hao Lu

Associate Professor at Huazhong University of Science and Technology, working at the intersection of computer vision and plant phenotyping.

Yadan Luo

ARC DECRA and Senior Lecturer at the University of Queensland. Her research advances the generalization and efficiency of 2D and 3D vision models.

Shouyang Liu

Professor at Nanjing Agricultural University and Distinguished Professor of Jiangsu Province, integrating computer vision, crop physiology, and quantitative remote sensing for digital agriculture.

Wei Guo

Associate Professor and Principal Investigator at The University of Tokyo, focused on AI-driven plant phenomics, field-based sensing platforms, and smart agriculture.
